Welcome to Gainz App

Created for hodler's who need some clarity

Gainz App: A Cryptocurrency cost basis tool that's offline and private.
Gainz Mission Statement: Help people solve the "I don't know what I've done with my crypto" problem :-)

For many of us in the crypto community there comes a day when we are faced with trying to make sense of an exchange csv.
These come from places like Coinbase, CashApp, Kraken, pretty much anyplace you've bought or sold crypto.

These CSVs consist of buys, sells, sends, and receives that likely don't tell the whole story for the purpose of finding taxable events and associated cost basis.
This can seem nearly impossible to figure out without some help.

One option is to use a service like Cointracker where you upload your CSVs or connect the service to all your accounts giving the 3rd party potentially sensitive information.

Gainz was made to be used offline and disconnected from the internet, so you can connect all the dots and work with scenarios that meet your needs without sacrificing privacy.

Gainz will import exchange transactions CSV(s) then guide you though some common scenarios to provide clarity and insights into your crypto holdings and associated gains or losses.

Get started by importing transactions then work your way down the left column to export.

Reports will be exported in excel format for your reference and shareability.
